Trip to Surtningssue

-Through fire and ice, through storm and shadow, we journey on...

Surtningssue stands as one of Norway's towering giants, an awe-inspiring peak that commands the title of Jotunheimen's most magnificent vantage point. The adventure begins at the Memurubu Tourist Cabin, where the well-marked trail winds ever upward, guiding intrepid souls to the summit.

A picture of a sign

There are two paths to conquer Surtningssue—one through the majestic Memurudalen Valley or over the rugged Raudhamran. For the bold, a glorious loop awaits, encompassing both routes.

A picture of the top, to the right side

The quickest ascent to the peak leads you over Raudhamran, a steep 9-kilometer climb that rewards the brave with a spectacular early glimpse of the world below. This path is the most demanding, but the view from the top is nothing short of breathtaking.

Alternatively, choose the gentle embrace of the lush Memurudalen, where the trail rises gradually, and the 12-kilometer trek to Surtningssue unfolds at a more forgiving pace.

For those seeking a grand adventure, embark on the full circuit—ascending Raudhamran first and returning via Memurudalen. This 21-kilometer journey offers a perfect blend of challenge and beauty.

As you rise over Raudhamran, the landscape quickly transforms into vast, windswept highlands, and soon, the peaks of mountains towering over 2000 meters reveal themselves in all their glory.

The first stretch of the hike gently ascends to 1520 meters, where the terrain begins to soften. Beyond Hesttjønne, the path traces the ridge to Raudhamran, clearly marked with sturdy stones guiding you upwards. Continue on, dropping to point 1791 meters on the map, then ascend toward 1910 meters, where the trail from Memurudalen converges. Here, the final, steeper push to Surtningssue awaits, marked by cairns and well-worn paths. At 2230 meters, a refuge hut—your sanctuary against unforgiving weather—stands ready to offer shelter. Remember, take care to leave no trace and close the door behind you.

Beyond this point, the terrain eases, and the summit beckons. Take your time and savor every moment as you approach the pinnacle of Surtningssue, where the world unfurls beneath you in a panoramic masterpiece.

The descent from Surtningssue is just as stunning. Wind your way down into the fertile Memurudalen, following the tranquil river Muru as it rushes toward Gjende, with the mighty glacier stream beside you.

This journey to Surtningssue is no mere hike—it's a grueling 9-10 hour odyssey, a true test of endurance and spirit.
